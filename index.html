Willinton Triana Welcome to my creative space. My work is deeply rooted in a multi-layered mindset and framework design, drawing inspiration from the profound poetry of Rainer Maria Rilke and the inventive brilliance of Leonardo da Vinci. Alongside these artistic influences, I possess a unique set of knowledge in sentiment analysis within large language models (LLMs), enabling me to bridge the gap between creative expression and advanced AI technologies. Through this blend, I aim to craft innovative solutions that merge creativity, precision, and depth.

I'm a self-taught on design developer who specializes in creating not so famous brands and websites for new companies. As a hands-on creative developer, I'm enthustiast on collaborating with my clients mixing ideas functionality and aesthetics. I bring together experience from across disciplines to help my clients earn their spot in people’s lives.
